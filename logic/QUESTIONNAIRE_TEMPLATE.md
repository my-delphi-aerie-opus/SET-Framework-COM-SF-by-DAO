### 📉 The Output Spectrum (The "Organism" Profile)
The final aggregated score across the 9 dimensions generates a **Vector**.

1. **Classic Alignment:** Score aligns with established patterns (e.g., Federated Hub).
2. **Hybrid Alignment:** Score sits at the intersection of two archetypes (e.g., Sovereign-Mesh Hybrid).
3. **Emergent/Non-Conforming:** Score resides in an "Outlier" zone. This indicates a requirement for a **Novel Operating Model**—a bespoke structure necessitated by unique Socio-Econo-Technical pressures.
